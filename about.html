About the Project:

Protecting the Pages: A visualization of contemporary book banning:

Book banning has become an increasingly serious problem across the US, sparking issues of freedom of speech, parental rights, and representation of diversity. The study of banned books and the cultrue around book banning may help to reduce harm. This project seeks to make information about book banning more accessible and interactive through public, digital media.
